about | terri

My name is Terri Nakamura and I'm an award-winning graphic designer, content creator, writer, editor and social media consultant.

I've been recognized for my social media influence and engagement. I've collaborated with companies including Verizon and The Consumer Technology Association–who brings us “CES,” the Consumer Electronics Show, each year.

I've been blogging since 2005. In addition to my own properties, I micro-blog on Instagram once a week and I've contributed to blogs in the U.S., Canada, U.K., and Italy.

key learning experiences

At Bellevue College I've learned about Marketing/Digital Marketing; Research; Analytics; Social Media; International Marketing; Content Management; Content Creation and Curation; Advertising; Content Strategy for B2B, B2C and Nonprofits; SEO; and Mobile Marketing; Music Marketing, and HTML and CSS. I've earned certifications in Hubspot Inbound, Content, and Email; Hootsuite Social Marketing; Google Mobile Ads and Search Ads; and Facebook Blueprint.
